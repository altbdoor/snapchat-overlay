Snapchat Overlay

A simple Snapchat overlay. Let's snip.

Most of the Snapchat overlay websites I see are either riddled with advertisements, or require you to upload the image into their servers. Well, yes, one can claim to not save a copy of your image through a thousand worded terms and conditions page. And along with it, "the terms and conditions is subject to change without notice".

So, with a "hey, maybe this could work" mindset, I started making a draft with a DOM-to-image library, only to notice that webfonts are not supported. I later found the Canvas API as an alternative, and voila. All the rendering work is done by your browser. None of the image information is being sent to anywhere (unless your browser does it).

This web application is only tested on Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome.

The font "Lato" by Łukasz Dziedzic is used, as its being claimed to be the closest font to Avenir, which is said to be the font face being used in Snapchat.

A large part of the controls are left to the user, as a user might upload images of different sizes, and I am too lazy to calculate the appropriate sizes for the overlay items for different image sizes.

Found an issue? Report it on GitHub.
